Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025 · Applies to footballandfighting.com

This Privacy Policy explains how Football & Fighting ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses and protects your personal data when you visit our website or use our services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are

Football & Fighting is operated as a sole trader business based in the United Kingdom. For data protection purposes, we are the data controller for personal information collected through this website.

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, contact us at: privacy@footballandfighting.com

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Name and email address when registering for an account or joining the waitlist
  • Payment information when purchasing a membership (processed securely by our payment provider — we do not store card details)
  • Any content you post in the community, including threads, replies and predictions
  • Communications you send us via email or contact forms

Information collected automatically

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited, time spent on site and referring URLs
  • Device type and operating system
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 7)

Information from third parties

  • Payment processors (Stripe) may share transaction confirmation data
  • If you sign in via Google or another provider, we receive basic profile information you authorise

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide our services — managing your account, delivering membership content and processing payments
  • To send you updates — where you have opted in, sending tips, previews and newsletters by email
  • To communicate with you — responding to enquiries and providing customer support
  • To improve the site — analysing usage patterns to improve content and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations — maintaining records as required by law
  • To prevent fraud — detecting and preventing fraudulent activity or abuse of our services

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contract — processing necessary to deliver the membership and services you have purchased
  • Legitimate interests — improving our service, preventing fraud and analysing site usage
  • Consent — marketing emails and non-essential cookies, where you have given explicit consent
  • Legal obligation — where we are required to process data by law

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Payment processors (Stripe) — to process membership payments securely
  • Email service providers — to send transactional and marketing emails
  • Analytics providers — to understand how the site is used (data is anonymised where possible)
  • Legal authorities — where required by law or court order

All third-party providers are required to handle your data securely and in accordance with applicable data protection law.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you cancel your membership, we will retain your data for up to 2 years for legal and accounting purposes, after which it will be securely deleted.

Community posts you make may remain visible after account deletion unless you specifically request their removal.

7. Cookies

We use cookies to make the site work and to understand how it is used. These include:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function (login sessions, preferences)
  • Analytics cookies — to understand traffic and usage patterns (only set with your consent)
  • Marketing cookies — only set with your explicit consent

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via your browser settings. Refusing non-essential cookies will not affect your ability to use the site.

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests or for marketing purposes
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@footballandfighting.com. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

9. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss or disclosure. These include encrypted data storage, secure payment processing via Stripe and restricted access to personal data within our systems.

No transmission over the internet is completely secure. While we take all reasonable steps to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Affiliate Links

Football & Fighting contains affiliate links to bookmakers and other services. When you click an affiliate link and sign up or make a purchase, we may receive a commission. This does not affect the price you pay. All affiliate relationships are clearly disclosed. Our editorial content and tips are never influenced by affiliate arrangements.

11. Children

Our services are not intended for anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page and notify registered members by email of any significant changes. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, contact us at:
