Plain English Summary

This site has no advertising network, no Facebook pixel, no Google Analytics, no third-party tracking scripts. Your browser fetches sports data directly from ESPN and The Guardian RSS. We do not see or store that data — it goes from their servers to your screen. If you create a membership account, we store your email address to manage your subscription and nothing else.

1. What We Collect

Visitors with no account

If you visit this site without creating an account, we collect no personal data about you whatsoever. There are no analytics scripts, no advertising trackers, no fingerprinting tools. Your IP address is processed by Cloudflare as part of serving the page, but we do not have access to server-level access logs for individual visitors.

Membership accounts

If you create a membership account, we collect:

  • Email address — to identify your account and send you content you opted into
  • Username (chosen by you) — displayed on the community forum
  • Password (hashed, never stored in plain text)
  • Subscription status — whether you have a free or paid membership

We do not collect your real name, phone number, date of birth, or payment card details. Payment is processed by Stripe, who have their own privacy policy — we receive only a token confirming payment was made.

2. Third-Party Data Sources

This site fetches live sports data directly in your browser from the following sources:

  • ESPN API — football fixtures, standings, player stats, UFC events. Your browser makes a direct request to ESPN's servers. ESPN's privacy policy governs any data ESPN records about that request.
  • The Guardian RSS (via rss2json.com) — football and boxing news headlines. Your browser fetches from rss2json.com, which converts the RSS feed to JSON. No personal data is sent in these requests.
  • Google Fonts — we load the Bebas Neue and Inter typefaces from Google's CDN. Google records this request as part of their standard CDN operation.

We have no control over what these third parties record. All of these fetches happen client-side — your browser makes the request, not our servers. We do not proxy or log the responses.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

We set no tracking or advertising cookies.

We may use browser localStorage to remember your preferences (for example, which football league tab you had selected last, or your preferred dark/light theme if we add one). This data never leaves your device — it is not sent to our servers.

Cloudflare may set a technical cookie (__cf_bm) as part of their bot management service. This is a security measure, not a tracking cookie, and expires within 30 minutes.

4. Hosting & Infrastructure

This site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages, a static site hosting platform. Cloudflare processes requests to serve the pages and provides DDoS protection. Cloudflare's privacy policy governs the data they handle as infrastructure provider. We do not receive individual access logs.

The site is entirely static HTML, CSS and JavaScript. There is no database on our side, no server-side processing and no session state for non-members.

5. Membership (If You Sign Up)

If you create a free or paid membership account:

  • Your email address is stored securely to manage your account
  • Paid subscriptions are processed by Stripe — we never see your card number
  • You can request deletion of your account at any time by emailing us
  • We will not send you unsolicited emails. Any emails we send will be content you specifically opted into (e.g. a weekly tips digest)

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR and similar frameworks, you have the right to:

  • Know what data we hold about you
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@footballandfighting.com. We will respond within 30 days.

For visitors with no account, there is effectively no personal data to act on — we hold nothing about you.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. We will never make changes that introduce tracking or advertising without clearly communicating this.

8. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at hello@footballandfighting.com or use the community forum.
