The Founder

Hi. I'm Chris.

I've been obsessed with football and boxing for as long as I can remember. The Premier League, European nights, world title fights — these aren't just sports to me, they're a way of life.

For years I consumed sports media the same way everyone else did. But something always frustrated me — the analysis was shallow, the tips had no reasoning behind them, and the data that actually explained what was happening was buried or locked behind expensive subscriptions.

I wanted a place where genuine analysis met real data. Where tips came with the reasoning, not just the pick. Where football and boxing fans could talk to each other properly — not shout into the void on social media.

So I built it. Football & Fighting is everything I wanted as a fan and couldn't find anywhere else. I run it myself, I write the analysis, I make the tips — and every piece of it is driven by real knowledge and real data, not filler content and clickbait.

The Mission

Football & Fighting exists for one reason: to give fans the analysis, data and community they actually deserve — without the noise, the paid promotion or the vague hot takes that pass for insight everywhere else.

Every piece of analysis is data-backed. Every tip comes with the reasoning. Every claim is something I'm prepared to stand behind publicly — because the tips tracker is right there on the site for everyone to see.

"I don't want to be another tipster shouting picks into the internet. I want to be the person whose reasoning you trust — win or lose."

The Creator tier exists because I also believe that fans who love these sports should be able to build something from that passion. If our data and content tools help a football or boxing creator grow their channel, that's something I'm genuinely proud of.

This is a long-term project. The goal is to build the most trusted, most data-driven football and boxing platform in the UK — and eventually far beyond it.

What We Stand For

📊

Data Over Opinion

Every claim we make is backed by data. xG, form, head-to-head records, punch stats — we show our working. Anyone can have a hot take. We show you why.

🔍

Full Transparency

Our tips record is public and verified. Every win, every loss, every void — right there on the site. We don't cherry-pick. We don't hide bad weeks. You deserve the full picture.

🚫

No Paid Promotion

We don't take money to promote bookmakers or products we don't believe in. Affiliate links are clearly disclosed. Our tips are ours — they're never influenced by who's paying.

🤝

Community First

The best conversations about football and boxing happen between fans who genuinely know their stuff. We're building a community worth being part of — not a comments section.

Responsible Gambling

Football & Fighting features betting tips and odds. We take this responsibility seriously. Our tips are for entertainment purposes only and should never be treated as financial advice or a guaranteed income.

Never bet more than you can afford to lose. Gambling should be fun. If it stops being fun — or if you feel like you're chasing losses, betting to relieve stress, or hiding your gambling from people close to you — please reach out for help.

All tips are published with full reasoning and a verified public record. We are transparent about our losses as well as our wins. We never encourage reckless staking and always recommend a sensible, flat-stake approach.
