About Us

We built Football & Fighting because we wanted one place for everything — live scores, deep stats, UFC cards and boxing news — without the clutter, paywalls or ads that slow every other sports site down.

What We Are

Football & Fighting is an independent sports site covering the Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A, Ligue 1, the Champions League, the FIFA World Cup 2026, UFC and professional boxing. Everything updates automatically throughout the day using live data feeds.

Live Data — How It Works

Every page pulls real-time data directly in your browser from ESPN and The Guardian. Scores, tables and fight results are as fresh as possible at all times.
